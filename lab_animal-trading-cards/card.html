Squirrel

Jan. 21 is National Squirrel Appreciation Day. In Greek “squirrel” means “shadow tail".

  • Scientific Name: Sciuridae
  • Average Length: 3-36 inches (7-91 cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 15-18 years
  • Habitat: Wooded areas, on every continent except Antarctica and Australia

The clever Sciuridae family (highly varied with 285 species, like the flying squirrel) were purposely introduced to US city parks to create “a bucolic atmosphere”. To prevent wear from gnawing on nuts, their front four teeth continue to grow throughout their lifetime. When threatened, squirrels run away in a zigzag pattern (a strategy to escape predators), but it also leads to discombobulated drivers!
